Frequently Asked Questions

How to use UCare?

To access UCare's feature, you can use the homepage or the dropdown menu at the top of the screen.

How to use UCare's News function?

UCare's News function is visible from the homepage and dropdown menu. The only step you need to take is clicking on the button. Then, a list of up-to-date news will be displayed. You can read news, save it for later, or simply scroll through them when bored.

How to use UCare's Skills function?

UCare's Skills function is visible from the homepage and dropdown menu. The only step you need to take is clicking on the button. Then, a list of skill categories will be displayed.
